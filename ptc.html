About Company

Founded in 1990, Persistent Systems is a global software services company headquartered in Pune, India.

Core Focus: Digital business acceleration, cloud migration, and product engineering services.

Clients: Serves Fortune 500 companies in healthcare, banking, and technology sectors.

Future Vision: Leading in AI-powered enterprise solutions and sustainable IT practices.

  • Basic knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
  • Database management skills (SQL/NoSQL)
  • Good understanding of SDLC
  • Communication & teamwork abilities

Placement Criteria

  • Minimum 6.0 CGPA
