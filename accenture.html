About Company

Founded in 1989, Accenture is a global professional services company specializing in IT services, consulting, and outsourcing.

Core Areas: Digital transformation, cloud migration, strategy consulting, and AI integration.

Global Presence: Operates in more than 120 countries, serving clients across all major industries.

Future Vision: Leading in AI-driven consulting, sustainable tech solutions, and global innovation hubs.

Skills Required

  • Basic programming skills (Java, Python, C++)
  • Problem-solving and logical reasoning
  • Good understanding of SDLC and Agile
  • Communication and teamwork skills
  • Adaptability to new technologies

Placement Criteria

  • Minimum 6.5 CGPA

Interview Questions
