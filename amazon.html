About Company

Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has evolved into one of the world’s largest e-commerce and cloud computing companies.

Amazon Web Services (AWS): Amazon is not just about e-commerce; it also leads the global cloud computing market through AWS, providing solutions for businesses of all sizes.

How Amazon Works: Amazon focuses on customer obsession, operational excellence, and innovation. It operates in e-commerce, digital streaming, AI, and logistics while maintaining a strong emphasis on technology.

Future Vision (Next 5 Years): Amazon aims to expand in AI-driven automation, global logistics, and cloud computing, making services faster, more secure, and sustainable.
