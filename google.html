About Company

Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google started as a search engine and has grown into a global technology leader.

Core Areas: Search, advertising, Android, YouTube, cloud computing, and AI research.

Culture: Known for innovation, employee-friendly work environment, and cutting-edge research.

Future Vision: Focused on AI-first solutions, quantum computing, and sustainable technology initiatives.

Skills Required

  • Strong programming in C++, Java, or Python
  • Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Machine Learning fundamentals
  • System design & scalability concepts
  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking

Placement Criteria

  • Minimum 7.0 CGPA
  • No active backlogs
  • Strong problem-solving track record (LeetCode, Codeforces, etc.)
  • Multiple interview rounds: Online Test → Technical Rounds → HR

Interview Questions

  • Implement a Trie data structure.
  • Design a scalable URL shortening service.
  • Find the median of two sorted arrays.
  • Explain PageRank algorithm.
  • Describe a challenging bug you fixed.
