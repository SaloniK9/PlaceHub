About Company

Founded in 1981 in Pune by N.R. Narayana Murthy and colleagues, Infosys grew from a consulting startup to one of the top global IT firms, reaching over 320,000 employees by 2025.

Infosys is a global leader in digital services and consulting, offering end-to-end business solutions.A Infosys is multinational corporation with a strong presence in over 56 countries. Infosys provides a wide range of services, including consulting, digital transformation, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence

Current Status: With revenues above USD 14 billion in FY25 and presence in over 56 countries, Infosys leads in AI, cloud, consulting, and digital transformation across key sectors. It continues strategic collaborations such as ongoing expansion with Citizens Financial in cloud-native, AI-first banking solutions.

Recent Moves: Infosys has reaffirmed its no-layoff policy in 2025 and aims to hire 20,000 freshers, while reskilling over 275,000 employees in GenAI and enterprise platforms. Its acquisitions include Germany-based in‑tech (₹450 crore) in 2024 and Australia-based MRE Consulting in 2025.

Future Outlook (2026–2030): Infosys plans to scale agentic AI and small language models, strengthen consulting-led digital offerings, and invest in ESG-led growth aligned with its 2030 ESG Vision including climate-positive commitment and skills enablement initiatives.
