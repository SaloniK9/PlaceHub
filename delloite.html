About Company

Founded in 1845, Deloitte is one of the "Big Four" accounting and professional services firms, offering consulting, audit, tax, and advisory services.

Core Areas: Business consulting, technology integration, risk management, and analytics.

Presence: Operates in over 150 countries with clients across multiple sectors including finance, healthcare, and technology.

Future Vision: Becoming a leader in data-driven consulting and AI-powered business strategy.

Skills Required

  • Programming skills (Java, Python, SQL)
  • Business and data analysis skills
  • Understanding of ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
  • Problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Strong communication and presentation skills

Placement Criteria

  • Minimum 6.5 CGPA
  • No active backlogs
  • Strong analytical and business understanding
  • Process: Online Test → Case Study Round → Technical/HR Interview

Interview Questions

  • Explain the difference between INNER JOIN and LEFT JOIN in SQL.
  • How would you analyze financial data for trends?
  • Write a program to check for prime numbers.
  • Describe a business problem you solved using technology.
  • What do you know about Deloitte’s consulting services?
