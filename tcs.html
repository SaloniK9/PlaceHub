About Company

Founded in 1968, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a part of the Tata Group, India's largest multinational business group. It started as a division of Tata Sons Limited and has grown to become one of the world’s largest IT services and consulting companies.

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a leading global IT services, consulting, and business solutions organization. Headquartered in Mumbai, India, TCS operates in over 50 countries with more than 600,000 employees. It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group, one of India's largest multinational business groups. TCS focuses on digital transformation, providing services like application development, cloud solutions, cybersecurity, and more.

How TCS Works: TCS offers a range of services including software development, business solutions, and consulting. It operates in domains such as banking, finance, healthcare, and retail, with a client-centric delivery model and innovation-driven solutions.

Future Vision (Next 5 Years): TCS aims to lead digital transformation globally with a focus on AI, automation, cloud, and sustainability. It plans to expand its global reach while building a more agile, adaptive workforce.
